DIY Cyberdeck Build

Introduction

This project aims to build a custom DIY cyberdeck based on a DeskMini X300 barebones PC paired with an AMD Ryzen 7 5700G.

Progress

The project was heavily inspired by the r/cyberdeck subreddit, the novel Necromancer, and Cyberpunk 2077. I wanted to create a cyberdeck that emphasizes power and functionality over the typical Raspberry Pi-based designs, opting instead for a desktop-grade processor.

Hardware

  • DeskMini X300 and Ryzen 7 5700G with a custom 3D-printed bracket for better cooling.
  • 27,000mAh 100W power bank for portable power.
  • 100W USB PD trigger and high-quality USB-C cables.
  • Logitech Pebble Mouse and a slim generic Bluetooth keyboard.
  • PETG filament and neodymium magnets for enclosure design.

Enclosure Design

The enclosure is based on a Pelican-style waterproof box. Inside, a 3D-printed PETG structure houses the DeskMini motherboard and provides multifunctional storage for peripherals.

Power Solution

The cyberdeck is powered by a 27,000mAh 100W power bank, though battery life is limited. I'm exploring custom LiPo battery solutions to extend usage time.
